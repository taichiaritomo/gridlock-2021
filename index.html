GRIDLOCK

GRIDLOCK consists of two Japanese-American artists from New Jersey. Their music pays homage to their upbringings, blending elements from ’80s Japanese City Pop and American alternative. The jazz musician and Jersey club producer duo combine layers of soulful live instrumentals and steady electronic beats to create uplifting dance tracks that they call “slo-fi disco.”
